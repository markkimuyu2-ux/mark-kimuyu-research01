How it works

A process built around verification

Most of what goes wrong in research work is a number that doesn't trace back to its source. The process below exists to catch that before you see it.

01

Scope the brief

We agree on the dataset, sources, format, and deadline up front — no ambiguity about what "done" looks like.

02

Work the data

Analysis is run in R, Excel, or Python depending on the task, with intermediate outputs kept so any figure can be retraced.

03

Audit before delivery

Every statistic, citation, and formula is checked a second time against its source before the document is finalised.

04

Deliver & revise

You get a clean, formatted deliverable, plus one round of revisions if anything needs adjusting.
